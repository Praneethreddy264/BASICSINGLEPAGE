Famous Sports Personalities

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli (born 5 November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional medium-fast bowler. He currently represents Royal Challengers Bengaluru in the IPL and Delhi in domestic cricket.

Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest batters of all time. He also holds the record for most centuries in ODI cricket and is the highest run-scorer in IPL history. He was part of India's 2011 Cricket World Cup, 2013 Champions Trophy, 2024 T20 World Cup and 2025 Champions Trophy winning squads, and led India to win the ICC Test mace in 2017, 2018, and 2019.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese footballer who plays as a forward for Al-Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history, he has won five Ballon d'Ors and 34 trophies including five Champions Leagues and the UEFA Euro 2016.

He holds records for most goals and assists in the Champions League and European Championship, most international appearances (221), and goals (138). He has scored over 900 senior career goals.
